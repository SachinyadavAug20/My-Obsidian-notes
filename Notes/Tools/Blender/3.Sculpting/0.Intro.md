###### But the sculpted objects can rarely be used directly for animation. The messy and dense topology is too demanding for rigging and rendering. For that purpose we create what is called a "Retopology". This is a clean, lower density, remodeled object, based on the shapes that were sculpted. It might seem like double the work but it's much more efficient to tackle the design and the final animation-friendly topology as separate tasks.
###### The fine surface details and painted colors of the sculpt can also be "baked" into image textures for later use. It's a way to preserve all that work that already went into the design sculpt.
