### Standard material 
###### It is very versatile material and work for anything(![[Pasted image 20260427202357.png|101]],![[Pasted image 20260427202417.png|129]],...) and has many properties  ![[Pasted image 20260427202447.png]]
###### Properties
- Albedo -> color (base color of material) and texture
- Transparency -> can make it transparent
- Metallic --> how metallic it should look like
- Rougness -> reflection and scarterness
- Normal map ->mesh not affected can have details ![[Pasted image 20260427202808.png|424]]
- Ambient occlusin --> how much light will reach surface
- Emission -> does surface emit light ![[Pasted image 20260427203122.png|356]]
