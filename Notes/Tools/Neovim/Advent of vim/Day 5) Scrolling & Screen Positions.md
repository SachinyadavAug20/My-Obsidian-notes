## 📘 Scrolling & Screen Positioning

---

## 🔹 Scrolling Commands
- `Ctrl + d` → Scroll **half screen down**
- `Ctrl + u` → Scroll **half screen up**
- `Ctrl + e` → Scroll **down one line**
- `Ctrl + y` → Scroll **up one line**
- `Ctrl + f` → Scroll **one full screen down**
- `Ctrl + b` → Scroll **one full screen up**

---

## 🔹 Cursor Position on Screen (No Scrolling)
- `H` → Move cursor to **top (High)** of screen
- `M` → Move cursor to **Middle** of screen
- `L` → Move cursor to **bottom (Low)** of screen

---

## 🔹 Centering & Repositioning Lines
- `zz` → Center the **current line** on screen
- `zt` → Move current line to **top** of screen
- `zb` → Move current line to **bottom** of screen
