Sometimes it is difficult to find what you want to do in Blender. For this and upcoming lessons, you can always use `F3` to open the [**Menu Search**](https://docs.blender.org/manual/en/4.5/interface/operators.html?utm_source=blender-4.5.1-lts#bpy-ops-wm-search-menu) and type what you want to do.
